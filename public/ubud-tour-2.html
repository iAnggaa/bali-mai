White water rafting

Although there are many rivers in Bali, not all rivers are suitable for rafting trips. There are three river locations in Bali that are ideal for rafting trips. The river is the Ubud Ayung River, the Telaga Waja Karangasem River, and the Klungkung Melangit River. Every stream has pros and cons. However, for this page, we inform you about the arung Ayung river in Ubud Bali for rafting trips.

ATV ride

TV Ride Bali is an activity that uses four-wheeled motorbikes, usually used for agriculture now for recreation. The wheels are specifically modified for off-road environments, with a 250 cc engine capacity or a 350 cc engine. It is suitable for wet and off road terrain, reliable controls for maneuvering so ATV Ride is a tourist activity to test adrenaline, if you follow instructions and remain careful, ATV rides are guaranteed to be an unforgettable and exciting trip.

Tirta empul

Tirta Empul, meaning ‘holy water spring’ is actually the name of a water source located within the temple. The spring feeds various purification baths, pools and fish ponds surrounding the outer perimeter, which all flow to the Tukad Pakerisan River. Various sites throughout the region and many other archaeological relics relate to local myths and legends.

Gunung kawi temple

Gunung Kawi Temple complex, locally referred to as Pura Gunung Kawi, is one of Bali’s most unique archaeological sites, comprising a collection of ancient shrine reliefs carved into the face of a rock cliff. The main site overlooks the sacred Pakerisan River, which also flows by the Tirta Empul Temple a kilometre up north. Across the river from the ancient reliefs is a temple courtyard featuring old Hindu shrines in a more contemporary architectural style, which is attended by pilgrims especially during its ‘piodalan’ temple anniversaries.

Kanto lampo waterfall

When you are bored with the views of the beaches in Bali, visiting a waterfall in the Gianyar region, the Kanto Lampo Waterfall is an interesting choice. Unlike the location of a waterfall that is usually located in the mountains or highlands, Kanto Lampo Gianyar Waterfall is even in the lowlands and has a hot temperature during the day.

Tibumana waterfall

You will never get bored of traveling to Bali. In addition to the beach, there are alternative tourist attractions that are not less beautiful to visit. Like Tibumana Waterfall, whose natural beauty is also able to attract tourists to come. This waterfall is unique because of its shape like a twin waterfall.Tibumana Waterfall is a twin waterfall with 2 adjacent water streams. But when the river water above it is small, there is only one large stream that drops into a waterfall. Under the waterfall there is a pool of water that is not too deep so it is very comfortable if used for swimming or bathing. The manager has also prepared tires that can be used as buoys.
